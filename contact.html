Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about working with gavith

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple automations can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while complex system integrations may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. We can also provide training for your team and help with system updates and enhancements as your business grows.

What technologies do you work with?

We specialize in third-party platforms like Zapier, Airtable, Google Workspace, Shopify, WordPress, and many others. We choose the best tools for your specific needs and budget.

How do you handle data security?

We follow industry best practices for data security and privacy. We use secure connections, implement proper access controls, and ensure compliance with relevant regulations like GDPR and CCPA.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

We offer flexible support options including hourly consulting, retainer agreements, and project-based updates. We're here to help your systems evolve with your business needs.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise companies. We tailor our solutions to fit your budget and scale with your growth.
